IPL 2024

Qualified 1

The Kolkata Knight Riders (KKR) have qualified for the playoffs in the IPL 2024 season. They secured their spot after defeating the Mumbai Indians (MI) on May 11, making them the first team to reach the playoffs this season.

Qualified 2

Sunrisers Hyderabad (SRH) has qualified for the IPL 2024 playoffs. Along with SRH, the other teams that have secured a spot in the playoffs are Kolkata Knight RidersR), Rajasthan Royals (RR), and Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB)​.

Qualified 3

The Rajasthan Royals (RR) have officially qualified for the IPL 2024 playoffs. They secured their spot following Delhi Capitals' victory over Lucknow Super Giants, ensuring that RR will finish in the top four regardless of the outcomes of their remaining matches​.
